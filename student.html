Study tips

Space it out

Short, frequent sessions beat one long cram. Revisit each chapter a few days later to lock it in.

Recall, don't reread

Close your notes and try to redo the calculation or explain the idea from memory. Struggling is where learning happens.

Do the numbers

Management accounting rewards practice. Work problems by hand until the method feels automatic.

Understand the why

Know why a formula works, not just the steps — that's what lets you handle unfamiliar exam questions.

Keep an error log

Track the questions you get wrong and revisit them before the exam — your mistakes are your best study guide.

Teach a classmate

Explaining a topic out loud quickly reveals the gaps in your own understanding — and helps you both.
