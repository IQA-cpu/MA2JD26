Background

Mean Udam is a qualified professional accountant and a member of the Association of Chartered Certified Accountants (ACCA). He holds an MSc in Professional Accountancy from the University of London and is currently a Doctorate of Business Administration candidate at Grenoble École de Management, France.

At CamEd Business School he serves as Assistant Professor and Senior Internal Quality Assurance (IQA) Manager, teaching Management Accounting 1 (ACCT 221) and Statutory Auditing (ACCT 341). His work spans quality assurance, internal audit, research supervision and curriculum development, and he is active as an assessor for the ASEAN University Network (AUN-QA).
